Climber Concepts

Carbiner:
A tool used for belaying and clipping catch points
Pitch:
A section of a larger climb
Quickdraw:
A special tool for clipping into the mountain in case of a fall
Auto Breaking Device:
A belaying tool used to catch the climber in the event of a fall
Flashing:
A climber coloquialism for finishing a climb without relying on your partner.
